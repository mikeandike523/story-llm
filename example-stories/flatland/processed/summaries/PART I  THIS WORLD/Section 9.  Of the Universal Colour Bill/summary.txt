1. The decline of intellectual Arts led to the neglect of Geometry and related subjects, while the Art of Feeling faced similar disregard, resulting in increased insolence among the Isosceles classes.
2. As Soldiers and Artisans claimed equality with the highest classes due to Colour Recognition, they pushed for the abolition of aristocratic Arts and equal rights for all individuals.
3. Revolutionary leaders proposed a Bill to paint all classes, including Priests and Women, to symbolize equality, cleverly ensuring Women appeared similar to Priests, thus gaining their support.
4. The Colour Bill aimed to demoralize the Circles by introducing Colour, eroding their intellectual edge and ultimately paving the way for the downfall of the Aristocratic Legislature.
