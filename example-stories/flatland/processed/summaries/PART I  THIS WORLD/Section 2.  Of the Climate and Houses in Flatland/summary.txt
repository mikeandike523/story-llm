1. In the absence of celestial bodies, the inhabitants of this world determine direction using a natural attraction to the South, supplemented by rain patterns and structural orientations for guidance.
2. In temperate regions, the weak and aged, especially women, struggle with the southward pull, necessitating social customs that favor giving them the North side of the path.
3. The origin of light remains a mystery due to its uniform presence and failed investigations, leading to prohibitive laws against such inquiries.
4. Houses are typically pentagonal to prevent accidents, as square and triangular shapes are banned due to their sharp angles, with exceptions only for specific state buildings.
5. Over time, pentagonal houses became the norm for safety reasons, with square houses now rare and primarily found in remote areas.
